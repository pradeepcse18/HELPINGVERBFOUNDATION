Project 1A: Happy Diwali

There are so many people in villages who don't celebrate the festival of light. At the beginning of phase one, we decided to make this Diwali special for them. We gifted them a box of happiness which contained Sweets,lamps and sky lamps. So they can celebrate diwali with their loving people and family. They celebrated diwali. They were happiest



Project 1B: First Aid Box

There are so many people who live in such place where they have zero medical support nearby them. If someone gets sick in their family they need to go too far from home for the medical support. So if we give them the first aid kit and they apply the medicines at the beginning of the disease they can save themselves from getting caught in serious illness. They can stop the illness at very first place.
